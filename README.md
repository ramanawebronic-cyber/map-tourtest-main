## ğŸ”’ Privacy Policy

### Data Collection and Storage

Webronic 360 Tour is designed with privacy in mind:

- **No External Tracking**: The plugin does not send any user data to external servers or third-party services
- **Local Storage Only**: All tour data, images, and settings are stored locally in your WordPress database
- **No Cookies**: The plugin does not set any cookies for visitors viewing your virtual tours
- **No Analytics**: We do not collect analytics or usage statistics from your website visitors
- **GDPR Compliant**: The plugin does not process personal data, making it GDPR compliant by design

### User Data

- **Admin Users**: Tour creation and management data is stored in your WordPress database along with standard WordPress user information
- **Visitors**: No personal data is collected from visitors viewing your virtual tours
- **Media Files**: Uploaded panoramic images are stored in your WordPress media library with standard WordPress privacy settings

### Third-Party Integrations

- **Google Street View** (Pro feature only): When enabled, follows Google's privacy policy for Street View content
- **WooCommerce Integration** (Pro feature only): Follows WooCommerce's privacy policies when products are displayed in hotspots
- **Social Media Sharing** (Pro feature only): Sharing features connect to social platforms only when visitors actively choose to share

### Your Responsibilities

As the plugin user, you are responsible for:
- Ensuring you have proper rights to use any 360Â° images you upload
- Complying with privacy laws applicable to your website and region
- Including any virtual tour locations in your website's privacy policy if they contain personally identifiable information
